Learn About Common Eye Conditions

Explore detailed information about 8 major eye diseases. Click on any condition to learn about symptoms, causes, treatments, and management strategies.

Glaucoma

Optic nerve damage often caused by elevated intraocular pressure, leading to progressive vision loss.

Cataracts

Clouding of the eye's natural lens that develops gradually, affecting vision clarity.

Diabetic Retinopathy

Retinal damage caused by prolonged high blood sugar levels in diabetes patients.

Macular Degeneration

Progressive vision loss affecting the macula, the central part of the retina responsible for sharp vision.

Retinitis Pigmentosa

Genetic disorder causing gradual loss of light-sensitive cells, beginning with night blindness.

Conjunctivitis

Inflammation of the conjunctiva (pink eye), commonly caused by allergies, infection, or irritation.

Uveitis

Inflammation of the uvea (middle layer of the eye), causing pain and potential vision complications.

Keratoconus

Progressive eye disease where the cornea thins and gradually bulges into a cone shape.
