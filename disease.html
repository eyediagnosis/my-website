Disease Info

Learn about various eye conditions

Here you can find detailed definitions, symptoms, and possible actions for eye diseases.

Glaucoma

Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, often linked to high eye pressure.

Cataracts

Cataracts occur when the lens of the eye becomes cloudy, causing blurred vision and glare.

Diabetic Retinopathy

A diabetes complication that affects eyes, caused by damage to blood vessels in the retina.
